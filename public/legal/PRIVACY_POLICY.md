# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** May 18, 2026  
**Effective Date:** June 1, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Doctor Appointment App ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates the Doctor Appointment App website and mobile application (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our Service.

**IMPORTANT:** We are committed to HIPAA, GDPR, and CCPA compliance. Your health information is treated with the highest level of privacy and security.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information

**You provide directly:**
- Name, email address, phone number
- Date of birth, gender
- Medical history and health information
- Insurance information
- Payment information
- Emergency contact information

**Automatically collected:**
- IP address and device information
- Browser type and operating system
- Cookies and similar tracking technologies
- Usage patterns and interaction data
- Location information (if authorized)

### 2.2 Health Information (PHI)

We collect Protected Health Information (PHI) including:
- Medical history and diagnoses
- Current medications and allergies
- Lab results and imaging reports
- Appointment records
- Prescription information
- Treatment plans

**HIPAA Notice:** Your health information is protected under the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA).

### 3.1 Primary Uses
- Facilitating appointment scheduling

### 3.2 Legitimate Business Purposes
- Complying with legal obligations

- Detecting and preventing fraud

- ❌ Sell your personal information

**Healthcare Providers:**

**Service Providers:**
- Payment processors

### 5.2 Administrative Safeguards
- Access controls and authentication
- Audit logging of all data access
- Employee confidentiality agreements

- Secure data center facilities
